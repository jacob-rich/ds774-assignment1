Welcome

This is the official site of Rich Properties, a multifamily investment company based in Durham, New Hampshire. We excel at the acquisition, development, and management of multifamily assets ranging from duplexes to 200+ unit apartment communities.

The company has over $2B in real estate under ownership and management, making it the largest real estate investment firm in New Hampshire's Seacoast region.

At Rich Properties, we don't exist just to generate sizable returns for our investor-clients, but also to invest long-term in the communities in which our properties are located, by sponsoring community infrastructure projects and programming.
